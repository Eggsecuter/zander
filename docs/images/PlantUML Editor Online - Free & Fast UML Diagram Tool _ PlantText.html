Send the current diagram link to your own webhook. Supported services include Zapier, Hook.io, and IFTTT.

Details

There are many use cases for sending your diagram link to a webhook. For instance, you can use Zapier to save diagrams to Google Drive or email to your colleagues. Let your imagination run wild!

The webhook must accept the POST HTTP verb and take a JSON data payload in the body of the request. Take a look at the Request field below for details. And the Webhook URL must be secure (use HTTPS) and use a supported domain.
